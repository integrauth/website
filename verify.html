Verify a Certificate

Every IntegrAuth Academy certificate carries an ID like IA-20260716-92-4F7A2C9B. Enter the name and the certificate ID exactly as printed on the certificate to confirm they belong together.

The check runs entirely in your browser — nothing you type is sent anywhere. A valid result confirms the certificate ID was generated by the IntegrAuth Academy final exam for exactly this name, issue date and score. Capitalisation of the name doesn't matter; spelling and spacing do.
