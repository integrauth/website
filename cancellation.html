Cancellation and Refund Policy

Our subscription services are billed on a monthly or yearly basis. Subscriptions may automatically renew unless you cancel your subscription. You can cancel your subscription at any time to stop future charges.

If you wish to cancel your subscription and request a refund, you must provide a valid reason for the cancellation. We review refund requests on a case-by-case basis and reserve the right to decline any refund request.

To cancel your subscription, please log in to your account and follow the cancellation procedures, or contact our customer service team for assistance.

Please note that cancelling your subscription will not automatically issue a refund. Refunds are issued at our discretion based on the circumstances of each case.

If you have any questions about our cancellation and refund policy, please contact us at akhil@integrauth.com.
